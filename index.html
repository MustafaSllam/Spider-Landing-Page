Story
In "Marvel's Spider-Man 2," Peter Parker and Miles Morales team up to protect New York City while juggling personal challenges. Peter grapples with the symbiote's influence, while Miles navigates college applications and family life. A new threat emerges in Kraven the Hunter, who seeks worthy opponents, adding another layer of conflict as he targets supervillains.
